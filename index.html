About

I’m a UConn designer focused on typography, interaction, and environmental storytelling.

I like building crisp systems that leave room for play: modular grids, variable type, and small motion moments that guide—not distract. I’m currently exploring multi-script typography and information design rooted in Earth & environmental science.

Outside class, you’ll find me prototyping in code, designing posters, or contributing to student org projects (and apparently befriending lambs).
